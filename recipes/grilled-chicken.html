Grilled Chicken

Recipe Overview

Grilled Chicken is so simple, but it can be difficult to get just right. Follow this recipe and have your friends and family drooling.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 6 tbs extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 tsp lemon zest, from one lemon
